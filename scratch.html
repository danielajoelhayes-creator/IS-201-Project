About My Trips

I’ve been to Hawaii twice, and each trip was unforgettable! I explored the islands, tried local foods, hiked scenic trails, and enjoyed the beaches. This page shares highlights from both adventures.

Favorite Places & Activities

  1. Trip 1 (Year)
    • Stayed at the Royal Hawaiian Hotel
    • Experienced a crazy flash flood evacuation!
    • Visited Waikiki Beach
    • Went to Waimea Lookout next to Ke Nui Kitchen — stunning greenery views
    • Attended Toa Luau — fun traditional Hawaiian show
    • Explored North Shore & Haleiwa
    • Tried Haleiwa acai bowls
  2. Trip 2 (Year)
    • Visited Kahuku Food Trucks — amazing local eats
    • Attended “House Without a Key” for hula performance
    • Went on a scenic boat ride
    • Explored a local farmer's market
    • Relaxed on nearby beaches and enjoyed island vibes
